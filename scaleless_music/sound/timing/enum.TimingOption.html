Optional duration type, for timings in sequences.

Variants

None

Timing is turned off (= unlimited duration)

TimeConst(SampleCalc)

Constant amount of time independent of rhythm. For a sequence item, it is independent of the sequence's whole duration.

TimeRatio

Proportion relative to a sequence's whole duration (in time). The sequence's timing must be time based!
